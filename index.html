Assignment

Design a Marketing Playbook, as a web page, using the resources provided. Create the page in html/css/js and have it publicly accessible so we can review it.

Teams find Trello when looking for solutions to their problems. Our team has decided to create unique landing pages for various business teams teams like marketing, sales, HR, etc that show how Trello solves that team's unique issues. Think of these pages as "playbooks" for various departments on how they should consider using Trello with their team.

We would love to see you design a Trello for Marketing Playbook! Use the requirements and content below, but feel free to add any graphics, screenshots, animations, quotes - whatever you feel adds to the value of the page (you can make any of these things up...and please do!).

Requirements:

The "Marketing Playbook" should include the following:

  • "Sign Up" CTA
  • "Request a Demo" CTA
  • All content listed here: https://trello.com/b/VqucIFCV/marketing-playbook
    Here you will find links to sample boards, recommended Power-Ups (advanced Trello features & integrations), related blog posts, etc.
  • Any visuals that assist in the goal of this campaign
  • Design that utilizes the branding guidelines & provided CSS

Assignment notes:

  • Placeholder copy is fine for this page.
  • When it comes to both design and code, improvise/add wherever needed.
  • Keep analytics in mind as you create the page. What will you measure, and how?
